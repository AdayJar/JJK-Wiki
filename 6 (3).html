Jujutsu Kaisen season 3: release date speculation, trailer, and everything we know so far

"Jujutsu Kaisen" is a popular Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. It was first serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump in March 2018. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who joins a secret organization of jujutsu sorcerers to eliminate a powerful curse named Ryomen Sukuna after becoming his vessel. The series has gained widespread popularity due to its intense action, deep characters, and unique curse-related storyline.

Jujutsu Kaisen season 3 is officially on the way and, while it's still early, we know the hit anime will adapt the manga's Culling Games arc.
Whether you're a newcomer or well-versed in the source material, we're here to guide you through the essentials and other confirmed news surrounding Jujutsu Kaisen season 3.
On top of that, we've got our very own release date speculation, a look at the very first teaser trailer, a glance ahead to which manga chapters it could adapt, and the list of cast members who may (or may not) return.

Jujutsu Kaisen season 3 story: what’s happened and what chapters will be adapted next?

The end of the Shibuya Arc saw ‘Geto’/Kenjaku awaken several non-sorcerers (those who had ingested cursed objects or had latent abilities of their own, including a certain Tsumiki Fushiguro). That’s all part of a plan to have sorcerers and non-sorcerers kill each other. Later, Gojo is exiled from Jujutsu society, with Yuji being sentenced to death – and swordsman Yuta (who you may remember from the Jujutsu Kaisen 0 movie) named as his executioner.
