Main Characters Gege Akutami Anime Season 3 Popularity Ranking

Characters

Itadori Yuji
Yuji Itadori is a student at Jujutsu High who gains cursed energy powers after consuming Sukuna's finger.
Role: Student at Jujutsu High
Rank: Grade 1 sorcerer
Physical Strength: Exceptional
Cursed Energy: High, capable of using Black Flash
Notable Skills: Host of Sukuna, quick reflexes, strong determination
Gojo Satoru
Gojo Satoru is a teacher at Jujutsu High and one of the strongest Jujutsu Sorcerers.
Role: Teacher at Jujutsu High
Rank: Special Grade Sorcerer
Physical Strength: Extraordinary
Cursed Energy: Extremely high, capable of using Limitless Cursed Technique
Ability: Mastery of Limitless Cursed Technique and Six Eyes
Notable Skills: Immense combat prowess, mastery of Cursed Techniques, strategic intelligence
Fushiguro Megumi
Megumi Fushiguro is a student at Jujutsu High with a powerful Cursed Technique.
Role: Student at Jujutsu High
Rank: Grade 2 Sorcerer
Physical Strength: High
Cursed Energy: High, specializes in Shikigami Summoning
Ability: Mastery of Ten Shadows Technique, summoning Shikigami
Notable Skills: Strategic combat, strong Shikigami, skilled in Cursed Techniques
Kugisaki Nobara
Nobara Kugisaki is a student at Jujutsu High with a unique approach to Cursed Techniques.
Role: Student at Jujutsu High
Rank: Grade 2 Sorcerer
Physical Strength: Strong
Cursed Energy: High, specializes in Straw Doll Techniques
Ability: Mastery of Straw Doll Cursed Technique, uses nails and hammer
Notable Skills: Fearless combat style, strong resolve, effective Cursed Techniques
Okkotsu Yuta
Yuta Okkotsu is a former student at Jujutsu High and a powerful Sorcerer.
Role: Former Student at Jujutsu High
Rank: Special Grade Sorcerer
Physical Strength: Exceptional
Cursed Energy: Extremely high, wielder of Rika Orimoto’s Curse
Ability: Mastery of Rika Orimoto’s Curse, powerful Cursed Energy manipulation
Notable Skills: Immense cursed energy, formidable combat abilities, strong protective curse
Inumaki Toge
Toge Inumaki is a student at Jujutsu High who uses his Cursed Speech Technique to control others.
Role: Student at Jujutsu High
Rank: Grade 2 Sorcerer
Physical Strength: Moderate
Cursed Energy: High, specializes in Cursed Speech
Notable Skills: Cursed Speech Technique, ability to control and command others with his voice
Zenin Maki
Maki Zenin is a student at Jujutsu High known for her exceptional combat skills and physical prowess.
Role: Student at Jujutsu High
Rank: Grade 2 Sorcerer
Physical Strength: Exceptional
Cursed Energy: Moderate, relies on physical strength and weapons
Notable Skills: Mastery of combat techniques, strong physical abilities, proficient in cursed tools
Fushiguro Toji
Toji Fushiguro is a former Jujutsu Sorcerer known for his exceptional combat skills and cursed technique.
Role: Former Sorcerer
Rank: Special Grade Sorcerer
Physical Strength: Extraordinary
Cursed Energy: Low, utilizes physical prowess and cursed tools
Notable Skills: Mastery of combat techniques, skilled in using cursed tools, great strategic mind
Sukuna
Sukuna is a powerful Cursed Spirit and the King of Curses.
Role: Main Antagonist
Rank: Special Grade Cursed Spirit
Physical Strength: Extremely high
Cursed Energy: Extremely high, formidable Cursed Techniques
Notable Skills: Mastery of various Cursed Techniques, immense combat power, regeneration
Jogo
Jogo is a powerful Cursed Spirit known for his volcanic powers.
Role: Antagonist
Rank: Special Grade Cursed Spirit
Physical Strength: Very high
Cursed Energy: High, specializes in fire-based attacks
Notable Skills: Volcanic abilities, high durability, destructive power
Hanami
Hanami is a powerful Cursed Spirit who embodies nature.
Role: Antagonist
Rank: Special Grade Cursed Spirit
Physical Strength: Very high
Cursed Energy: High, uses nature-based attacks
Notable Skills: Plant manipulation, regenerative abilities, high resilience
Mahito
Mahito is a malevolent Cursed Spirit with the ability to manipulate souls.
Role: Antagonist
Rank: Special Grade Cursed Spirit
Physical Strength: High
Cursed Energy: High, specializes in body manipulation
Notable Skills: Soul manipulation, body modification, regeneration
