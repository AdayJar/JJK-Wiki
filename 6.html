Jujutsu Kaisen season 3: release date speculation, trailer, and everything we know so far

"Jujutsu Kaisen" is a popular Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. It was first serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump in March 2018. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who joins a secret organization of jujutsu sorcerers to eliminate a powerful curse named Ryomen Sukuna after becoming his vessel. The series has gained widespread popularity due to its intense action, deep characters, and unique curse-related storyline.

Jujutsu Kaisen season 3 is officially on the way and, while it's still early, we know the hit anime will adapt the manga's Culling Games arc.
